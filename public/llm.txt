# TRVERSE

> TRVERSE is a mass transit platform for automated fare collection (AFC), intelligent transport systems (ITS), fleet management, advanced scheduling, micro-mobility integration, and AI monitoring. Built for transport authorities, city governments, and private operators worldwide. Backed by LMKR.
